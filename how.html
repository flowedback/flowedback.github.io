First Step: Create an Umbrella-ID account

If you have not done so yet please create a new Umbrella-ID account. This account is your personal account and will survive any change of affiliation.

Create Umbrella-ID Account

Second Step: Connect your Umbrella-ID account with an Umbrella Service

To be able to use your account you need to connect it with your existing accounts at different facilities. Following link will give you a list with Umbrella-ID enabled services that allows connecting your Umbrella-ID account with the local facility account:

Find Umbrella-ID enabled facility

Third Step: You're finished and ready to use your Umbrella-ID account

If everything went fine you can now use your Umbrella-ID account to login at all of your facilities. Remember to connect new facility accounts with your Umbrella-ID account to enjoy all the advantages of Umbrella-ID.
