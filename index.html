Umbrella is a digital identity, developed for use by Photon and Neutron users. Globally recognised and secure.

Nice! How can I use it?

Umbrella is built on top of the existing local IT infrastructures and it links its identity in a once-only action directly to the local identity at the respective facility.

Great! Where can I use it?

Umbrella can be used at following facilities: ALBA, DESY, DLS, Elettra, ESRF, ILL, ISIS, KIT, MAX IV, PSI, SOLEIL and Instruct.
